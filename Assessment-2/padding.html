Padding inline - Padding-inline creates gap in the left and the right side.

Padding block - Padding-block creates gap in the top and the bottom side.

Padding inline end - Padding-inline-end creates gap in the right side of the content.

Padding inline start - Padding-inline-start creates gap in the left side of the content.

Padding top - Padding-top creates gap only in the top side of the content.

Padding right - Padding-right creates gap only in the right side of the content.
